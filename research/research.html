HOME   ABOUT   RESEARCH  


I study adversarial machine learning from a learning theory perspective. My supervisors James Worrell, Varun Kanade and Marta Kwiatkowska and I recently published our work on the sample and computational complexity of robust classification. I am particularly interested in identifying thresholds for robust learning, namely when can efficient robust learning be guaranteed.
