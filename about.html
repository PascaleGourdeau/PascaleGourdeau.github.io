I was born and raised in Montreal, still living in the city, one of the greatest, most exciting place to be!

Before attending university, I went to Lester B. Pearson UWC of the Pacific for two years. a boarding school part of the United World Colleges movement. The UWC mission is to make education a force to unite people, nations and cultures for peace and a sustainable future. There are no words to express how transformational this experience was. I met incredible individuals with whom I developed family-like bonds, became a better version of myself, built the foundations for a fulfilling life, discovered passions, etc.
