I am born and raised in Montreal, and still living in that wonderful city -- one of the greatest and most exciting place to be!

I am now in my third year of studying Computer Science at McGill University, with a minor in Mathematics. In my last two years, I’ve enjoyed every course I’ve taken, loved participating in hackathons, did a little bit of research, and the verdict has come: I can’t see myself studying anything else!

Before attending university, I went to Lester B. Pearson UWC of the Pacific for two years. a boarding school part of the United World Colleges movement. The UWC mission is to make education a force to unite people, nations and cultures for peace and a sustainable future. There are no words to express how transformational this experience was. I met incredible individuals with whom I developed family-like bonds, built the foundations for a fulfilling life, discovered passions and ultimately became a better version of myself.
