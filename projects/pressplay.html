I attended my first Hackathon, McHacks, with Michael Noseworthy, Rachel Bergmann and Brendan Gordon last winter. Together, we developed a website that lets the user enter the SoundCloud usernames of the people they are hanging out with and generates a playlist based on their tastes.

Our team finished in the top 10 out of more than 80 teams, and about 500 participants!
