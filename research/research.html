I am doing research in Machine Learning medical applications in the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University. I am under the supervision of Pr Doina Precup and Pierre-Luc Bacon, a PhD student.

I am working to develop and test algorithms to analyse medical time series and perform classification on data sets. The ultimate goal of the project is to help physicians determine when extreme preterm infants are ready for extubation.

Here is the poster I presented at the McGill Undergraduate Research Conference in October. Keep an eye out for the one I will present at the Women in Machine Learning Conference this December!
