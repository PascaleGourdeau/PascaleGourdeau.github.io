My main research interests are machine learning and automata theory. I am currently working on medical applications of machine learning and on bisimulation for weighted finite automata in the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University. I will start my Masters in May, under the supervision of Pr Doina Precup and Pr Prakash Panangaden.
