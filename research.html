I am doing research in Machine Learning medical applications in the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University. I am under the supervision of Pr Doina Precup and Pierre-Luc Bacon, a graduate student.

I am working to develop and test algorithms to analyse medical time series and perform classification on data sets. The ultimate goal of the project is to help physicians determine when extreme preterm infants are ready for extubation.

You can find some of the code I wrote for the project here.
